Who are you when your job title disappears?

Most professionals don’t realise how much of their identity was built around their career — until the meetings stop, the structure disappears, and nobody is asking for their input in quite the same way.

This is not just a retirement question.

You’ve spent decades building expertise, influence, reputation and rhythm. Then retirement arrives — and the external structure that held much of your identity in place quietly falls away.

The challenge is rarely just financial. For many experienced professionals, the deeper question is: what gives life meaning, momentum and relevance now?

There are only two types of retirement.

Default Where life slowly loses structure, energy and direction.
Designed Where you deliberately shape your next chapter with purpose.
